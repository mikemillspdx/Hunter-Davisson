GIVING BACK


Hunter-Davisson has a long history of giving back to our community. We do this in honor of our founder, Joe Davisson.

Every year we choose a charity that is near and dear to our hearts. We hold an annual giving campaign where employees support local charities through payroll contributions that are matched by Hunter-Davisson. Each year as our team grows, so does our giving.

This year we have chosen to give to Compassion First. This non-profit organization helps provides long-term, hope-filled solutions for survivors of sex trafficking and exploitation in Indonesia.
