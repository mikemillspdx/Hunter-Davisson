Community

Hunter-Davisson has a long history of giving back to our community. In addition to corporate giving, we host an annual giving campaign where employees support local charities through payroll contributions that are matched by Hunter-Davisson in honor of our founder, Joe Davisson. Our employees also give generously of their time and HVAC skills to many charities.

  • Boys & Girls Society
  • DePaul Youth Treatment Center
  • Doernbecher Children’s Hospital
  • Good Samaritan Ministries
  • Innovative Housing, Inc.
  • Loaves & Fishes
  • Mercy Corps
  • NW Housing Alternatives
  • Oregon Alzheimer’s
  • Oregon Food Bank
  • Our Peaceful Place
  • Portland Youth Builders
  • Reach Community Develop
  • St. Vincent de Paul Emergency Services
  • Wheels to Walk Foundation
