Commercial Controls

Building Management Control systems should improve the comfort of your working environment, lower your HVAC operating cost, and allow you to easily monitor the performance of your equipment. If your control system doesn’t do all three with ease, let our team of Engineers and Technicians help you achieve your mechanical system goals. Hunter-Davisson is an approved Honeywell Authorized Controls Integrator (ACI) with over 25 years of experience providing digital automation solutions and services to meet our customer’s energy management and comfort needs.



By providing open-sourced digital control software that is accessible via the web or through mobile applications, Hunter-Davisson’s engineered applications will keep HVAC equipment communicating through open protocols such as BACnet, Modbus and LON to provide electronic alarm notification, trend data analysis reporting, and energy monitoring through graphical interfaces customized to the customer’s specifications.


Our staff has experience in installing and servicing Honeywell, ProLon, Pelican, Trane Tracer, JCI, Carrier, Walker controls and more. As a local contractor serving the Portland Metro area since 1965, you can depend on Hunter-Davisson to be your long-term automation DDC partner to help you efficiently operate your mechanical systems.
