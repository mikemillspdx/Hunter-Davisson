History

Harold Hunter and Joe Davisson first opened Hunter-Davisson on December 1, 1965. From day one, our philosophy has been based on the Golden Rule: Do unto others as you would have them do unto you. For customers and suppliers alike, we have always stood for quality design, superior equipment, expert installation and outstanding service. Our projects are fairly priced and we have always shown the highest respect for our industry and its practices. Call us, and experience how these traits are woven into the fabric of Hunter-Davisson, and modeled by our hand-selected staff.
